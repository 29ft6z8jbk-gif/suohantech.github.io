About Us

Founded in 2016, Shanghai Suohan Technology Co., Ltd. is a convergent media system service provider operating in the fields of radio & television equipment technology and intelligent technology in Shanghai. Powered by computer technology, the company is a convergent media equipment supplier specializing in digital audio/video software, hardware, and system integration. Our business scope includes but is not limited to: wholesale and retail of electronic products, photographic equipment, communication equipment, lighting equipment, audio equipment, monitoring equipment, display devices, etc.; computer information system integration; stage design and construction; and design and construction of decoration and renovation engineering projects.

Since its inception, the company has become an authorized dealer for many well-known domestic and international brands, establishing solid partnerships across the domestic media industry. The ultra-high-definition (UHD) broadcasting systems designed, constructed, and commissioned by our technical team have delivered outstanding performance during live broadcasts and transmissions for major events, including the UEFA Euro 2020, the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics, and Dragon TV's flagship IP show "Chinese Idol".

From mobile photography and social media Vlog/Plog content creation to variety shows, TV series, film production, news gathering, major sports events, gala networked broadcasting, and virtual production imaging solutions;

From personal and home audio entertainment products like headphones, digital audio players, and soundbars to professional audio recording and monitoring equipment and solutions;

From home display solutions such as BRAVIA TVs and INZONE gaming monitors to Crystal LED, BRAVIA commercial displays, projectors, professional film/TV production monitors, medical-grade monitors, and Spatial Reality Displays widely used in commercial, enterprise, education, design, transportation, and cultural tourism sectors;

Based on content creation experience and technological expertise accumulated across the "Lens to Living Room, Recording to Listening" industry chain, we deeply understand creators' vision, delivering high-quality, efficient workflows and cutting-edge solutions that faithfully reproduce creative intent and inspire audiences.

Service Areas

TV Stations & Convergent Media

Offering comprehensive product services for TV stations, supporting 4K/8K UHD studios, OB vans, professional recording/monitoring equipment, and IP Networked Live solutions. Guided by media convergence, we leverage broadcast-grade equipment to deliver complete media solutions spanning studio live broadcasting, mirrorless camera news gathering, and conference reporting.

Sports Events

Filming solutions for sports events, featuring 4K/8K UHD system cameras, shoulder-mount broadcast cameras Z750/Z580, 4K handheld camcorder Z280V, 4K professional monitors, switchers, and shallow depth-of-field cinematic additions like 4K PTZ camera FR7, handheld cinema cameras FX6/FX3, and Alpha 7 series mirrorless cameras. Multi-camera setups combined with agile single-camera units allow flexible switching and high mobility in tight spaces (such as player tunnels). Full-frame sensors deliver exceptional low-light performance and dynamic range for cinematic close-ups.

Film & TV Production

Providing solutions for movies, TV drama series, variety shows, reality TV, esports, and live event broadcasts. From system broadcast cameras to cinema cameras, we empower the film and TV production industry with diverse workflow solutions.

Educational Institutions

Product solutions covering everything from news gathering and monitoring equipment for media majors to film/micro-movie production, documentaries, commercial shooting, campus publicity, conference recording, smart classrooms, campus info distribution, and cloud classroom streaming.

E-Commerce Live Streaming

Tailored options for medium-to-large live events, celebrity live streams, and compact store broadcasting setups with multi-camera configurations. Combined with professional microphone setups, we ensure high-definition visual and audio quality. As live streaming becomes a vital channel for consumer interaction on e-commerce and social platforms, our setups deliver real-time engagement and premium visual experience.
