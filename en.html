About Us

Established in 2016, Shanghai Suohan Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. is a premier converged media system service provider operating in the fields of broadcast television technology and intelligent technology in Shanghai. As a computer-technology-driven enterprise, we specialize in supplying digital audio/video hardware and software equipment, alongside comprehensive end-to-end system integration solutions. Our business scope encompasses the wholesale and retail of professional electronic products, photographic equipment, communication infrastructure, lighting systems, audio systems, monitoring utilities, and display arrays, as well as computer information system integration, stage structural design, and decoration engineering construction.

Since our inception, we have become an authorized partner and signed agent for numerous world-renowned domestic and international brands, maintaining robust collaborations with premium partners across the media industry. The ultra-high-definition (UHD) broadcasting and OB van systems designed, integrated, and commissioned by our technical engineering team have delivered outstanding technical performance for landmark live broadcasts, including the 2020 UEFA European Championship, the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympic Games, and Dragon TV's flagship IP variety show "The Voice of China."

Imaging & Production: From mobile photography, social media Vlog/Plog workflows to variety shows, drama series, cinematic creations, newsgathering, sports broadcasting, large-scale galas, and cutting-edge virtual production imaging solutions.

Audio Engineering: Comprehensive audio portfolios spanning personal/home entertainment setups (headphones, digital audio players, soundbars) to high-end professional audio recording, monitoring gear, and matrix routing infrastructure.

Display Solutions: Professional display arrays from commercial screens, Crystal LED displays, and high-end projectors tailored for corporate, educational, hospitality, and cultural tourism sectors, to reference-grade master monitors utilized in medical and film editing workflows.

Our Philosophy: Leveraging cumulative technical expertise built from "lens to living room, and recording to listening," we deeply understand creators' intents, providing high-efficiency, reliable workflows to faithfully replicate creative visions and move global audiences.
