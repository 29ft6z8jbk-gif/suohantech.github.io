About Us

Established in 2016, Shanghai Suohan Technology Co., Ltd. is a practical convergent media system service provider in Shanghai, specializing in broadcast television equipment technologies and intelligent tech solutions. As a provider focused on computer software/hardware and digital audio/video integration, our business scope includes: wholesale & retail of electronic products, photography equipment, communication devices, lighting gear, audio equipment, surveillance, and displays; computer system integration; stage design & construction; and decoration engineering design & construction.

Since inception, Suohan has authorized dealerships with multiple world-renowned brands and strategic media partnerships across China. Systems designed, integrated, and commissioned by our technical team delivered outstanding performance during major live broadcasting events such as UEFA Euro 2020, Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics, and Dragon TV's flagship show "Chinese Idol".

From mobile photography, Vlog/Plog content creation to broadcast series, film productions, news gathering, mega sports events, and virtual production solutions;

From headphones, digital audio players, and soundbars to professional audio recording and monitoring equipment and integrated solutions;

From BRAVIA TVs to commercial display solutions, Crystal LED, projectors, professional monitors, and Spatial Reality Displays across corporate, education, design, and healthcare sectors;

Leveraging deep technical experience "from lens to living room, from recording to listening", we faithfully render creator intent with high-efficiency workflows, bringing inspired experiences to audience worldwide.
