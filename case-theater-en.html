Migu Video UHD PQC Quality Control Center

Project Type: Broadcast-Grade Quality Monitoring Integration | Client: Migu Video Technology Co., Ltd. | Delivery Year: 2026

Project Overview: This project is the Ultra-High-Definition Production Quality Control (PQC) Center built for Migu Video Technology Co., Ltd. Unlike traditional content review systems, this center serves as a core technical hub dedicated to rigorous monitoring of UHD broadcast signals, transmission security, and underlying technical parameters. The system integrates advanced audio analysis-capable waveform monitors, 5.1.4 monitoring suite (AQC) workstations, and high-level data analysis video (VQC) workstations, ensuring technical compliance and broadcast safety for every single UHD video frame.

1. Monitoring Center Real-Shot Gallery

The PQC monitoring hall adopts a modern, ergonomic console design equipped with professional multi-viewer screen walls and a precision 5.1.4 audio monitoring system. The multi-viewer split-screen system enables duty operators to gain real-time insight into the waveforms, vectors, and live broadcast visuals of multiple signals. Additionally, a VoIP remote intercom system is integrated to facilitate latency-free communication with frontline production teams.

2. Core System Architecture and Equipment Selection

The system infrastructure adopts a high-redundancy, non-blocking signal routing and measurement architecture, with key technical nodes utilizing the highest broadcast industry standards:

Core Matrix & Routing

  • ROSS Ultrix Platform: Utilizes the ULTRIX-NS-FR1 core processing frame, offering highly integrated routing, multi-viewing, and signal processing capabilities.
  • ROSS 20x20 Signal Routing: Ensures seamless switching and routing allocation for 20x20 channels of 4K/UHD signals.

Quality Monitoring & Analysis

  • LEADER Test Instruments: Deploys advanced waveform monitors such as Leader LV5600/Qxp for real-time analysis of eye patterns, jitter, HDR color space, and audio loudness.
  • Telestream Monitoring: Integrates MPS-300 for deep signal compliance and streaming media analysis.

VQC / AQC Professional Workstations

  • SONY Top-Tier Monitoring: VQC workstations deploy SONY PVM-X2400 4K HDR master monitors, delivering mastering-grade color benchmarks.
  • Audio Acoustic Monitoring: Standardized with Genelec 5.1.4 monitoring speakers, providing an unrendered, precise audio evaluation environment.

3. Project Value

Through the successful deployment of this PQC Quality Control Center, Migu Video has achieved standardized technical interception and image quality grading for massive volumes of UHD content. The combination of AQC audio monitoring systems—equipped with advanced audio analysis-capable waveform monitors and 5.1.4 monitoring suites—and VQC video monitoring systems with high-level data analysis mechanisms utilizes real-time streaming pull for frontline signals, coordinated with localized baseband matrices and multi-viewer systems. This not only significantly enhances video quality monitoring efficiency but also establishes a robust quality and technical defense line for subsequent 8K/4K UHD major sports live broadcasts and on-demand services.
